Projects

The Wind Turbine Blades Design

This is one of my IB scholar subject's investigation during high school. I had a chance to design and build turbine blades, while also got a grasp on how fluid dynamics work. It was my first time making contact with complicated electrical components inside the motor so I really enjoyed it.

The Claw Design

This is one of the projects we did for a course during university. I worked mainly as a designer and assembler for an autonomous claw. I also contributed a small portion to arranging and presenting our posters.

The Claw Design (Part 2)

Using Arduino, we programmed to make it become a fully autonomous claw. The claw can pick up various designated objects by itself with a coded distance detecting system. The video is captured by Jerry Xu, one of my teammates.

My Own Personal Website

This website itself is made from scratch and it was my first interaction with HTML, CSS and Javascript. Even though the code is really not fancy, this experience helped me to build up some extra technical skills. In addition, the website allowed me to displayed more projects of mine and explained them in details, things I could not do in my resume.

Discord Command Bot

This is a little Discord bot that my friend and I developed in Python 3.6.9, using the discord.py library. The bot is able to stream music from Youtube videos and do Wolfram Alpha queries and so many other fun things. This would make online group work easier, more enjoyable without having to switch between tabs from Discord to Internet browsers to do such stuffs.

Remote Controlled (RC) Car

Two of my friends and I did this RC Car totally from scratch as first-hand experience. This is the first time I applied what I have learned in my Electrical related class, including Arduino coding, wiring and schematic drawing, into a real-life project. We encountered many issues as a result. One considerable problem was that Arduino could not deliver enough power to our RC Car, so I had to retrieve power from multiple ports. The RC car worked in the end and this opportunity gave me a lot of experience, especially about planning stuff beforehand.

Line Follower Car

After the making of that RC Car from scratch, another friend of mine and I decided to buy a tool kit in order to make the product a higher quality. I acted as the lead software developer and helped with some technical hardware designs due to my previous experiences. In addition, I learned to solder and grasp the basic concept of sensor designs through this project!

Simple RISC Machine

I built this in one of my courses along with a partner over the span of 4 weeks. We implemented the hardware design with Verilog, tested using ModelSim and synthesized it with Quartus. Through this experience, I managed to gain deep and tacit understanding of Microcomputers and Digital System Design.
